Problem Statement
Problem Statement

Today's digital age, the market for social apps is oversaturated with an abundance of platforms that encourage hookup culture and shallow connections. Unfortunately, this emphasis can impede individuals from finding authentic friendships within their respective communities, leaving them feeling disconnected and unfulfilled.

Millennials, who spearheaded the social media era, are increasingly reliant on online sources to meet and connect. However, this shift has also led to a rise in social anxiety and isolation, making it challenging for them to form meaningful friendships and a sense of community – especially when online engagement is often superficial.

"A poll of about 1,200 people by YouGov found 27% of Millennials have no close friends, and 22% report they have no friends at all. Most people find it difficult to make friends because they’re shy (53%), but people also report friendship is too much work (20%) or they are too busy (14%)." Forbes (2021)

Solution

BuddySystem is a unique platform that strives to create a welcoming environment for everyone. With an "all-inclusive" approach, the platform is designed to facilitate authentic friendships and vibrant communities through engaging events and shared interests.

User Research

Our team executed a series of 5 interviews with individuals from the target demographic (i.e. millennials ages 27-42) to gather qualitative data. The interviews were conducted virtually, and lasted between 20-45 min each. To ensure consistency, researchers used a semi-structured format for the interview. All interviews were recorded and transcribed for the future. Additionally we conducted a survey and used secondary data (i.e. via online sources) to inform our research and overall plan.

User Insight

Friendly Frankie needs a simple way to expand her circle of friends in order to better her social well-being because her experience becoming an entrepreneur has been extremely isolating, leading to unfulfillment in her personal life.

User Persona: Friendly Frankie

“I love my job as a freelance web designer, but I can’t be friends with my clients!”

  • Age: 30
  • Zodiac: Gemini
  • Occupation: Freelance Web Designer
  • Location: Toronto, ON (5 years)
  • Status: Currently in a long-term relationship

Friendly Frankie is a new entrepreneur working freelance at her own web design agency. Her career change keeps her busier than ever, with an unpredictable schedule. Her social circle is fairly small. She tends to spend most of her downtime with her partner but is craving platonic friendships. Residing in Toronto, her cost of living has been on a steady incline, so she’s taken up growing her own herbs on her condo balcony in hopes of saving a little money. She’d love to develop more of a green thumb and connect with others who could share their expertise.

Roadmap

There is a need - millennials do in fact want to engage, but social apps are very limited. It’s important to consider the nuances between dating and friendship app modules as expectations can be very different. Therefore BuddySystem should consider and reflect those differences in it’s design. Branding and marketing is essential in communicating the purpose of BuddySystem. This way we can mitigate users signing up for the wrong reasons. Onboarding is important in sustaining the purpose and ultimately controlling the users experience by incorporating capability features that speak to the NEED: friendship and community building.
